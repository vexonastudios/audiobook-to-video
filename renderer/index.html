How to Use Audiobook to Video

This tool turns your audiobook audio files and book cover into an engaging, animated YouTube video. Follow these steps to get started:

(0:00) Introduction
(1:45) 1 - The Journey Begins
(45:20) 2 - The Sea of Darkness

Click Parse Chapters to validate them. You will see a list appear!

4. Adjust Styles & Preview (Center Panel)

Use the sliders on the left to adjust background blur and opacity. You can also pick a Transition Style (like Fade, Cross Dissolve, or Zoom Blur) and set its duration. Use the Preview section's dropdown to see exactly what each chapter will look like.

5. Render Video

Once your fields are filled and chapters are parsed, click Render Video. The app uses your NVIDIA RTX GPU (NVENC) to encode the video in hardware — massively faster than CPU encoding! Choose H.264 for max compatibility or H.265 HEVC for ~40% smaller file sizes. The 🎮 GPU badge in the export bar confirms hardware acceleration is active.
